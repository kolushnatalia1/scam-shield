Suggested Screenshot Flow

Take screenshots in this order so the store listing tells a clear story:

  1. Hover a low-risk link and capture the calm tooltip.
  2. Hover a medium-risk link and capture the changed score/reasons.
  3. Hover a high-risk link and capture the high-risk tooltip.
  4. Click a high-risk link and capture the warning modal.
  5. Open the extension popup and capture suspicious-link history.
Tip: zoom browser to 110% or 125% if you want larger, easier-to-read labels in screenshots.
